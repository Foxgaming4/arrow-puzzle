How to play

Tap an arrow to slide it off the board in the direction it points.

An arrow can only leave if nothing blocks its path to the edge. If it's blocked, it gives a little shake.

Clear every arrow to win. Stuck? Use Shuffle to rearrange, or a Hint to spot a free move.

Tip: on desktop you can use arrow keys to move the cursor and Enter to tap.
